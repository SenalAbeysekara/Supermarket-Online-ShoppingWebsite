Online order & delivery related

Can I place an order for a person residing elsewhere within the delivery grid?

+

Yes, you can place an order for a recipient other than yourself by stating the delivery address and details accurately prior to checkout.

How do I know my order is confirmed?

+

Your order will be confirmed via email or SMS with the details provided.

Is there a delivery charge for online orders?

+

Delivery charges may apply based on the order amount and location. Details will be provided during checkout.

Is there a restriction on how many orders I can place per day?

+

No, there is no restriction on the number of orders you can place per day.

What happens if you make the delivery to the incorrect address?

+

The telephone number of the receiving party is a compulsory field in the order form. We will call and confirm the address and the route before delivering the order and any inaccuracies in the address can be mentioned assuming the address is within the delivery grid of the store the order was placed to.

How long does it take for the delivery once I place the order?

+

If you place the order before 11a.m (Sri Lankan time) we will deliver the order to you within the same day and orders placed after 11a.m (Sri Lankan time) will be delivered before 8p.m(Sri Lankan time) the next day. Our team will contact the receiver of the order prior to delivery to ensure the time is convenient for him/her. You will receive an email and/or SMS when the order is ready to be delivered. The above is applicable only when there is no disruption to standard operating hours due to any external environmental changes. This can change without prior notice if curfew is imposed.

Can I add additional products or change my order once placed?

+

This facility is not available at the moment.
